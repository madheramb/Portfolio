About Me

Hello there!

"I’m Heramb, an UI/UX Designer based in New Hampshire, USA. I am a creativity enthusiast and try portraying the same in all my work. I enjoy building and designing interfaces that are handy at the same time have their own uniqueness that reflects wholesomeness.

To mention a little about my academics, I graduated from Texas A&M University, Kingsville, Texas with my major in Computer Science. While studying and exploring different subjects, I worked as a Graphic Designer at the Office of Marketing and Communications at my university. I was also a part of a 24hour Hackathon organized by the University of Dallas sponsored by JP Morgan Chase and many others, where I and my team won first place for building an application called Helping hands. This application assisted the needy in times of emergencies and crisis, it served as a central platform for those in need of help and also for those willing to offer some.

I worked as a UI/UX Designer for CCA Global Partners in Manchester, NH for nearly two years. My work on several projects involved assisting with the Design, Development, Marketing Research, and working with other teams to develop user-centered features, Designing mobile and device compatible websites (Responsive Web Designing), Designing, developing, and testing HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and JavaScript that meets accessibility and web browser standards for the website, Cooperating with developers, product managers, QA, and marketing to generate incredible user interfaces and Create mockups and wireframes using Adobe Illustrator and Sketch.

At present, I am on a look for refreshing work that challenges me and inspires me to create interfaces and designs that would serve the optimum and more with respect to accessibility, aesthetic, updated features and much more.

I can describe myself as an optimistic individual, who loves the work he does and believes that design is all about details and how it aids functionality.

Always a step forward, to create complicated problems into creative and winsome solutions."
